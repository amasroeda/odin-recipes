Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled eggs are a quick, protein-packed breakfast or snack. They’re soft, fluffy, and can be easily customized with herbs, cheese, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them cook for a few seconds.
  4. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, folding them over as they begin to set.
  5. Continue stirring until the eggs are softly set and slightly creamy.
  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
