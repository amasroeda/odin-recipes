Fufu Recipe

Description

Fufu is a smooth, stretchy staple food from West Africa, made by boiling and pounding starchy vegetables like cassava, yams, or plantains. It’s typically served with soups or stews and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cassava
  • Plantain

Steps

  1. Peel the cassava and plantain and wash
  2. Cut into small sizes
  3. Place the pieces in a pot, add enough water to cover them, and boil until soft
  4. Use a wooden mortar and pestle to pound the boiled cassava until smooth and stretchy.
