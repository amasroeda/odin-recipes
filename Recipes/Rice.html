Rice Recipe

Description

Boiled rice is a simple, versatile staple food enjoyed worldwide. It’s easy to make and pairs well with almost any stew, sauce, or main dish.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Place the rice and water in a pot and add a pinch of salt
  3. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and cover the pot with a lid.
  5. Let it simmer for 15–20 minutes until the water is fully absorbed and the rice is tender.
  6. Remove from heat and fluff with a fork before serving.
